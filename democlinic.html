Patient Intake Guidance

Help patients arrive prepared.

The page can guide patients on what to prepare before a visit, without collecting unnecessary sensitive details on the public form.

Before your visit

Bring ID, insurance details if applicable, medication list, and previous test results when relevant.

For online consultations

Use a private space, stable internet connection, and keep important documents nearby.

Privacy guidance

Avoid entering sensitive medical history into a general website form unless the clinic provides a secure intake process.

FAQ

Common booking questions.

Is the appointment confirmed after submitting the form?+

If a real embedded calendar is connected, the selected time can be confirmed automatically or reviewed by the clinic depending on the clinic’s booking rules.

Can patients book online consultations?+

Yes, if the clinic offers online consultations and the case is suitable for remote care.

Should patients send medical documents through the form?+

For privacy, the public form should avoid sensitive medical details. The clinic can provide a secure intake process when needed.

Can this page connect to Google Calendar?+

Yes. The page can embed Google Calendar appointment scheduling or another booking provider directly inside the booking section.
